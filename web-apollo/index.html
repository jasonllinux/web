Introduction

Apollo-Rescue was established in 2009. It belongs to Apollo RoboCup Team, Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China. As a newcommer, Apollo-Rescue participated in annual competitions of RoboCup since 2010. We take RoboCup Rescue Agent Simulation as a typical problem of large multi-agent systems, and our long-term goal is to do research in decision-making in emergency enviroment, path planning and other challenging projects in Artificial Intelligence.

RoboCup Rescue Simulation is a Large Multi-Agent System which its aim is to manage the disaster when an earthquake happens. RoboCup Rescue's main purpose is to provide emergency decision support by integration of disaster information, prediction and planning. RoboCup Rescue Simulation System is one of the prominent systems for AI and Multi-Agent researches.

  • Research Areas
- Large Multi-Agent Systems
- Decision Making Algorithms
- Task Allocation Methods
- Multi-Agent Coordination and Team Formation Methods
- Behaviour Modeling
